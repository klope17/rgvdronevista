Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2025

What we collect

When you submit the quote request form on this website, we collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your field acreage
  • Your crop type
  • Your preferred time to be contacted

We do not collect payment information, location data from your device, or any information beyond what you enter in the form.

How we use it

The information you submit is used only to respond to your quote request. We will contact you by phone to discuss your field and provide pricing. We do not use your information for any other purpose.

Who sees it

Your form submission is processed by Formspree, Inc., a third-party form service that forwards the data to our email. Formspree may store your submission on its servers. You can read Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard server access data such as IP addresses and browser types as part of normal hosting operations. You can read GitHub's privacy statement at docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

Data retention

We keep your contact information only as long as needed to complete your quote or booking. If you ask us to delete your information, we will do so and confirm by phone or email.

Children

This website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
